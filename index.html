Research, Supervised by Dr. Kerby Shedden, UMich

Specific slices were identified based on bottleneck distance of persistence diagrams, and their corresponding individual clinical variables were compared and analyzed.

The topological mode analysis tool was used to check whether the glomeruli in each slice showed clustering and its relationship with the clinical variables of the corresponding individual.

Detect if there is a roughly linear relationship appear among some of glomerular based on the Hough transform.
